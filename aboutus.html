Ashley Says...

"I enjoy offering encouraging, uplifting, and mainly scriptural-based home, office, and church decor. I have named my buisness based on my favorite Bible Verse, Psalm 18:3 - God is my refuge, my stronghold - Even when you are barely holding on by a thread, God is our stronghold. I hope you will find strength and encouragement through these decorative pieces. I want to offer decor that helps others promote and teach scripture to their families and as a gift to their friends and loved ones. I want every StrongholdScripts piece to be a unique and special gift that you will treasure." - Ashley Adams, Buisness Owner

About StrongholdScripts...

StrongholdScripts is a small business run by a southern mother and wife. As a member of the church, Ashley Adams has a passion for sharing the word of God and has paired this with her creative talents to make encouraging signs individual to you. StrongholdScrits aims to meet you where you are with what you need to decorate your home or office. Since this is a family-owned business in western Kentucky. Ashley hopes to be a light to the community around her by making individual signs for you.
